30 /*
31  * This is a 2 phases events. Here follows an explanation of the main events and transition between phases and sub-phases.
32  *
33  * The first phase is the EYE phase: the Eye of C'Thun is active and C'thun is not active.
34  * During this phase, the "Eye of C'Thun" alternates between 2 sub-phases:
35  * - PHASE_EYE_GREEN_BEAM:
36  * 50 sec phase during which the Eye mainly casts its Green Beam every 3 sec.
37  * - PHASE_EYE_RED_BEAM:
38  * 35 sec phase during which the Eye casts its red beam every sec.
39  * This EYE phase ends when the "Eye of C'Thun" is killed. Then starts the CTHUN phase.
40  *
41  * The second phase is the CTHUN phase. The Eye of C'Thun is not active and C'Thun is active.
42  * This phase starts with the transformation of the Eye into C'Thun (PHASE_CTHUN_TRANSITION).
43  * After the transformation, C'Thun alternates between 2 sub-phases:
44  * - PHASE_CTHUN_STOMACH:
45  * - C'Thun is almost insensible to all damage (99% damage reduction).
46  * - It spawns 2 tentacles in its stomach.
47  * - C'Thun swallows players.
48  * - This sub-phase ends when the 2 tentacles are killed. Swallowed players are regurgitate.
49  *
50  * - PHASE_CTHUN_WEAK:
51  * - weakened C'Thun takes normal damage.
52  * - This sub-phase ends after 45 secs.
53  *
54  * This CTHUN phase ends when C'Thun is killed
55  *
56  * Note:
57  * - the current phase is stored in the instance data to be easily shared between the eye and cthun.
58  */
